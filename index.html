Snake Game by Youssouf

Welcome to my first javascript/HTML5 game.
You have to feed Sam the snake by directing him toward food.
Use the directional keys
(Up, Down, Right, Left arrows)
to controle Sam.

Avoid touching the surrounding walls or you'll hurt Sam.
Also don't let Sam bite his tail that hurts too.
